"I felt a bit disappointed with this product, the Radiance Glow Face Mask, just because of its unique purple color. First, it contains Brazilian purple clay, which makes it unique in color, along with Kaolin clay, lactic acid for gentle exfoliation, and menthol for a cooling sensation. However, since lactic acid and menthol are at the bottom of the ingredient list, their effects are minimal. It claims to be de-tanning and brightening, but I didn’t notice enough brightening or de-tanning effects even after more than a month of use. My tan remained the same. I understand that you may not see results after just one application, which is why I wanted to review it. As you can see, my skin didn’t get significantly brighter. I find that the Conscious Chemist purple mask is much better."
