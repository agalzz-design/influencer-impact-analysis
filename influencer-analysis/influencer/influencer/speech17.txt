"This is the WHOOP, the fitness tracker that all Indian players are using. Even Ronaldo has one. It doesn’t have a display, and the battery life lasts from 3 to 5 days. But why do all players use it? Because of ICC rules, players can’t use smartwatches on the field. WHOOP is different from other fitness trackers because you can wear it on your wrist or even on your bicep or boxer. Apart from normal fitness and sleep tracking, it’s excellent for tracking strain and recovery. Unlike other devices, WHOOP isn’t bought outright but through a subscription plan. The device is free, but the subscription costs around ₹20,000 per year in India."
