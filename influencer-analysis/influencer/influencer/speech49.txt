

I bought a silver cleaning liquid to clean some old silver jewelry. The liquid was chemical-based, and I was instructed to use a plastic bowl. When I poured the liquid on my silver jewelry, I noticed an immediate change in color. My wife was very happy with the results—it looked brand new! I dipped the jewelry in the liquid for a while, and the tarnish was gone instantly without any rubbing or scrubbing. I used the same liquid for all my silver items. For safety, I used a brush to remove them after cleaning. After washing them with soap and water to remove any residue, the jewelry looked like it was just bought from the showroom. Now, all my old jewelry looks brand new!
